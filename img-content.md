## 03. z8037256871660 — READING • PART 5: TENSES (Thì) — Simple & Continuous

**Bài tập đầu trang:** 4. They ..... for this company for the last five years (→ have worked). 5. The plane ..... by the time we get to the airport (→ will have landed). GET READY: nối form S+had+Vpp / S+have/has+Vpp / S+will have+Vpp với Past/Present/Future perfect.

**Ngữ pháp — 1. SIMPLE TENSES:**
| | Past simple | Present simple | Future simple |
|---|---|---|---|
| Structure | S + was/were; S + V-ed (wasn't/weren't) | S + am/is/are; S + V/Vs/es | S + will be; S + will + V-inf |
| Time expression | Yesterday, Last, Ago | Every day/week; Always, frequently; If/when/as soon as/until + present simple | Next, In + time |

Ghi chú tay trong sách: *annual = hằng năm; lịch trình/thời khóa biểu cố định → dùng hiện tại đơn; near future: be going to + V / be + V-ing.*

**2. CONTINUOUS TENSES:**
| | Past continuous | Present continuous | Future continuous |
|---|---|---|---|
| Structure | S + was/were + V-ing | S + am/is/are + V-ing | S + will be + V-ing |
| Time expression | At this time yesterday; At 6 p.m. yesterday | Now; At the moment; Currently | At this time tomorrow; At 6 p.m. tomorrow |
